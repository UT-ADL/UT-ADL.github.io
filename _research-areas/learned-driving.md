The learned driving team investigates the use of machine learning to simplify autonomous driving software. Instead of hand-crafted rules, we train neural networks to drive directly from sensor inputs.

Our key accomplishment is the **Rally Estonia Challenge** — we gathered over 500 km of recordings from WRC Rally Estonia tracks across four seasons and used this data to train end-to-end driving models. We successfully demonstrated these models on real rally stages, making ours the **world's first self-driving car to drive on an official WRC rally stage**.

We research various approaches including imitation learning, reinforcement learning, and conditional driving models. Our work spans from simulation (VISTA, CARLA) to real-world deployment on our Lexus RX450h test vehicle.
