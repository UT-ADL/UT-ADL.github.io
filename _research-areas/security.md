Security risk management, including autonomous driving vehicles, is essential to any system development. For autonomous driving service providers, knowing what risks exist in the system and how they could be mitigated is necessary. Security risk management methods allow system stakeholders to manage the security risks within their systems. In this research, we apply different security risk management approaches to explore autonomous vehicle architectures, identify security risks and propose their countermeasures.

The research interests include security risk management, management of sensitive data, and secure system design of:
- Autonomous vehicle architectures
- Passenger-vehicle interactions
- Teleoperation systems and their architectures
- Smart parking solutions
- Car sharing solutions
- and similar...
