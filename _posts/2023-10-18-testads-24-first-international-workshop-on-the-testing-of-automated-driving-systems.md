An international collaboration is organizing the inaugural workshop focused on testing of Automated Driving Systems (TestADS). The event occurs in Toronto, Canada, coinciding with the 17th IEEE International Conference on Software Testing, Verification and Validation (ICST'24).

The workshop objective centers on assembling specialists addressing all dimensions of ADS software evaluation. Priority is given to disseminating academic and industrial research contributions, though practitioner accounts are similarly valued. TestADS endeavors to establish a collective of engineering professionals with expertise spanning Machine Learning, Software Engineering, and Robotics.

## Relevant Topics

- Approaches and instruments for evaluating modular ADS components including perception, positioning, and decision-making systems
- Approaches addressing particular quality factors like safety, usability, and security
- On-road assessment, controlled environment assessment, computerized assessment, and combined methodologies
- Quantification approaches for ADS safety at autonomous levels 4 and 5
- Creation of standardized testing frameworks for ADS safety evaluation
- Assessment of teleoperation-enabled ADS systems
