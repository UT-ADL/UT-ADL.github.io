Once we’ve put a new and untried piece of self-driving tech through its paces in the simulated environment, we’re confident enough to undertake experiments with the real car in the real world. But before we proceed to the actual streets of Tartu that make up our demo track, we do closed testing on the huge parking lot of the Estonian National Museum that is conveniently close to us. There we can shift around the HD map of the actual demo track on top of the flat surface of the parking lot, so that we can go through all sections and junctions of the demo track - step-by-step - in a physical but safe environment.
