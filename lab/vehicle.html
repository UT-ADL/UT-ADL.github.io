The Vehicle

The most obvious piece of hardware that we utilize is our Lexus RX450h. It’s not your average SUV, however, as it is equipped with all the sensors that are a prerequisite for basic autonomy. The car and hardware were adapted by AutonomouStuff and everything is running on Autoware.AI software.

Lidar

Lidar sensors measure the distance to objects at incredible speeds and with high precision. They work much like radar, using light rather than radio waves — the lidar signal is reflected off objects and the light that returns is then measured.

Camera

The two cameras keep an eye on traffic lights

Radar

The radar helps detect vehicles out in front and estimate their relative speed.

GNSS Device

The GNSS device (with a little help from the satellites) determines the coordinates of the car.

Computer

The computer and drive-by-wire system connect all the hardware and let them communicate to each other.
